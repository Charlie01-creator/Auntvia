Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend placing your custom cake order at least 48 hours in advance. For weddings and large events, we suggest ordering 2-3 weeks ahead to ensure availability. During peak seasons (holidays, graduation, wedding season), even more notice is recommended.

Can I make changes to my order after submitting?

Changes can be made up to 48 hours before your event date, subject to feasibility. Major design changes may affect the price and timeline. Once baking has begun (24 hours before delivery), only minor adjustments can be made.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 72 hours before the event will receive a full refund. Cancellations 48-72 hours before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable as we have already purchased ingredients and allocated time for your order.

Do you offer tasting sessions for custom cakes?

Yes! We offer tasting sessions for wedding cakes and large orders (50+ servings). The tasting fee is $25 which includes 4 flavor samples. This fee is waived if you proceed with your order. Contact us to schedule a tasting session.

What allergens should I be aware of?

Our kitchen handles wheat, dairy, eggs, nuts, and soy. We take precautions to prevent cross-contamination, but we cannot guarantee allergen-free products. For severe allergies, we recommend looking for a dedicated allergen-free bakery. We can accommodate some dietary restrictions with advance notice.
