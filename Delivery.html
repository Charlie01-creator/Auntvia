Important Delivery Notes

To ensure the best experience with your cake delivery, please note these important guidelines.

Delivery Preparation

Please ensure someone is available to receive the cake at the delivery address during the scheduled window.

Temperature Control

Cakes should be refrigerated upon arrival if not serving immediately. Keep away from direct sunlight and heat.

Weather Conditions

In extreme weather conditions, delivery times may be adjusted for cake safety. We'll contact you if needed.

Delivery Acceptance

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our delivery service.

Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?

Address changes can be made up to 24 hours before your scheduled delivery. A $5 address change fee may apply if the new location falls in a different delivery zone. Changes within 24 hours may not be possible as cakes are already prepared for delivery. Contact our customer service team at (123) 456-7890 for assistance.

What happens if I'm not home during delivery?

Our driver will attempt to contact you via phone. If no one is available, we will:

  1. Attempt delivery to a neighbor if you've provided permission
  2. Leave the cake in a secure, shaded location if safe (with your permission)
  3. Return the cake to our bakery for later pickup
A $15 re-delivery fee applies if we need to return. We recommend providing delivery instructions when ordering.

Do you deliver on weekends and holidays?

Yes, we deliver 7 days a week including most holidays. However, delivery schedules may be limited on major holidays (Christmas Day, New Year's Day, Easter Sunday). A holiday surcharge of $10 may apply on certain dates. Weekend delivery times may be extended due to higher demand. We recommend ordering early for weekend celebrations.

What is your delivery cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 48 hours before delivery receive a full refund. Cancellations 24-48 hours before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable as we have already prepared your cake and allocated delivery resources. In case of emergencies, please contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate your situation.

Can I track my delivery?

Yes! For all deliveries, you will receive:

  • Order confirmation email with estimated delivery window
  • Text notification when your cake leaves our bakery
  • Driver contact information 30 minutes before arrival
  • Delivery completion notification
For express deliveries, you'll receive real-time GPS tracking via a secure link.
