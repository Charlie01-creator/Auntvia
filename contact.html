Contact FAQs

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us.

What information should I have ready when I contact you?

To help us assist you quickly, please have the following ready:

  • For order inquiries: Your order number and delivery date
  • For custom cake questions: Event date, number of guests, and any design inspiration
  • For delivery questions: Your delivery address and preferred time window
  • For general questions: Your contact information and best time to reach you

Do you offer cake tastings and consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary cake tastings for wedding cakes and large orders (50+ servings). The tasting includes:

  1. Selection of 4 cake flavors with matching fillings and frostings
  2. 45-minute consultation with our wedding specialist
  3. Design portfolio review and inspiration discussion
  4. $25 tasting fee that is waived if you proceed with your order
Contact our wedding specialist Michael to schedule a tasting.

Can I visit the bakery without an appointment?

Absolutely! Our bakery is open to the public during business hours. Walk-ins are welcome for:

  • Cake pickups (with order confirmation)
  • Purchasing from our display case of ready-made cakes and cupcakes
  • Quick questions and basic consultations
For detailed custom cake consultations, wedding tastings, or meetings with specific team members, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can give you our full attention.

What's the best way to contact you for a custom cake quote?

For custom cake quotes, we recommend:

  1. Fill out our Custom Order Form on our website for detailed pricing
  2. Email us at custom@auntviacakes.com with your requirements
  3. Call us and ask for our custom design team
  4. Visit in person for a free initial consultation
Please include as much detail as possible: event date, number of guests, design inspiration photos, preferred flavors, and any special requirements.

Do you have a mailing list for special offers?

Yes! Join our Sweet Updates newsletter for:

  • Exclusive discounts and seasonal promotions
  • First access to new cake flavors and designs
  • Baking tips and celebration inspiration
  • Birthday freebies and loyalty rewards
Sign up in the footer of any page or ask at our bakery. We send 1-2 emails per month and never share your information.
